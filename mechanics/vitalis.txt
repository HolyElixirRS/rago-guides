> **__Vitalis__**

At the weekly special Vitalis $vitalis Vorago launches an orb to a random direction, spawning multiple Vitalii.

• The orb is launched towards a cardinal direction
• If Vorago $vorago does not get moved, the orb direction is always east, north or north-east
• Once walked, the orb direction can be anywhere
• For each player tanking the explosion, one Vitalis $vitalis will be prevented from spawning
• The orb deals soft typeless damage to every player blocking a spawn (damage can be reduced by defensives)

__Vitali:__

• Vitali have 20,000 life points
• They attack with melee, dealing damage every 1.2 seconds
• Vitali are acting as blockades, interrupting the line of sight and blocking movement
• Their targeted player changes frequently, unrelated to player aggression
